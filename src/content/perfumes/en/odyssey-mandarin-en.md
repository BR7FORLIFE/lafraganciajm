## Detalles

A modern and seductive fragrance that combines citrus freshness with a sweet and woody touch. Mandarine Sky opens with vibrant notes of mandarin, orange, and saffron, creating a fresh and energetic sensation. It then evolves into a warm heart of caramel and tonka bean, which adds a sweet and elegant touch. Finally, it settles on a sophisticated base of vetiver, cedar, and ambroxan, leaving a masculine, modern, and long-lasting trail.
