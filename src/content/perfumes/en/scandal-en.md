Sandalwood fragrance carries a rich and enveloping character that blends depth with subtle sweetness. It often reveals a velvety texture, where dry woody tones meet faint balsamic nuances and a whisper of spice. The scent evolves gently on the skin, creating a grounded yet refined atmosphere that feels both timeless and intimate. It is commonly associated with a sense of balance, warmth, and quiet sophistication.
